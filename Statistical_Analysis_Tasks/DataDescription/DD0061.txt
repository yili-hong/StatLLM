The dataset year_sales contains sales records for a company, TruBlend Coffee Makers, that distributes the coffee machines. 

The dataset contains the following values from left to right:

Month: the month in which a sale was made
Quarter: the quarter of the year in which a sale was made
SalesRep: the name of the sales representative
Type: the type of coffee maker sold (standard or deluxe)
Units: the number of units sold
Price: the price of each unit in US dollars
AmountSold: multiplying Units by Price.
