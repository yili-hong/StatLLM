3. Attribute Information:

Variables
pulse
Type: Numeric
Description: Represents the pulse rate of an individual, measured in beats per minute (bpm).
Range: 20 bpm to 47 bpm
Units: Beats per minute (bpm)
Example Values: 27, 29, 34, 44
group
Type: Categorical (Numeric)
Description: Indicates the group membership of each individual. There are six groups, labeled from 1 to 6.
Categories:
1: Group 1
2: Group 2
3: Group 3
4: Group 4
5: Group 5
6: Group 6
Example Values: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6
