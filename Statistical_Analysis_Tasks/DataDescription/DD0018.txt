Assume that each of several test subjects is afflickted with tinea pedis (athlete's foot) on each foot, and each subject is given a treatment X on one foot and Y on the other foot. Frequency count from matched pairs of foot is measured for both cured and not cured. 
 
The subject column gives the id of individuals.
The X column gives the outcome of one foot, which is categorical and has two levels.
The Y column gives the outcome of the other foot, which is categorical and has two levels.

The dataset is named as athelete.
